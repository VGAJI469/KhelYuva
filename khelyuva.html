About KhelYuva

Revolutionizing sports talent discovery through AI-powered technology

Project Background

KhelYuva was born out of the Smart India Hackathon 2024 with a vision to democratize sports talent discovery across India. Our platform addresses the critical challenge of identifying and nurturing sports talent in rural and semi-urban areas where access to professional coaching and assessment tools is limited.

Using cutting-edge AI and computer vision technology, we provide automated fitness assessments that can be conducted anywhere with just a smartphone. This eliminates geographical barriers and makes talent discovery accessible to athletes from all corners of India.

Our mission is to create a level playing field where every athlete, regardless of their location or economic background, has the opportunity to showcase their talent and get discovered by scouts and coaches.
